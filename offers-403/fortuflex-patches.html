Fortuflex patches

The patch is designed for people who want to maintain joint health. The patch is 100% natural and helps to restore the mobility of the joint and maintain its health.

The natural ingredients in its composition act through the skin thus protecting the liver from unnecessary load. The patches are easy to use and very effective. They help to restore the function of the joints. It improves the metabolic processes, occurring in the joint, and protects it from the damage.

Fortuflex patches

The components also relive inflammatory processes in the joint and improve its elasticity. It can be used by both sportsmen and ordinary people, suffering from joint health problems or willing to protect their joints from the aging processes.

Ingredients

Glucosamine Is a natural component of joint tissue that helps repair cartilage, joints and ligaments. Glucosamine contributes to the formation of a full-fledged elastic and resilient cartilage of the joints, prevents the development of inflammation in the tissues of the joints. It enhances the formation of joint fluid that fills the joint.

Chondroitin Is a substance that is part of the cartilage tissue, ligaments and bones. It retains moisture, participates in metabolic processes, and stimulates the production of synovial fluid.

Cetyl myristoleate Helps to increase joint mobility. Increases the elasticity of cartilage tissue, increases the elasticity of the walls of blood vessels and connective tissues. Promotes the regeneration of cartilage and connective tissue, has an anti-inflammatory effect.

Vitamin C Participates in the synthesis of collagen, which strengthens blood vessels, ligaments and bones.

Use

Apply to undamaged skin. After 24 hours, remove and replace with a new patch in a slightly different position. Duration of use - not more than 30 days.
