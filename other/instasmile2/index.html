    The Process

    1. Take Your Smile Assessment

    Answer a few quick questions, and well help you choose an instasmile thats right for you.

    2. Order Online

    Once you know which instasmile you want, place your order online securely.

    3. Keep An Eye on Your Phone & Email

    Well message you after placing your order & keep you updated along the way.

    4: Take Your Impression

    Your impression kit will arrive in the post. Carefully follow the instructions, and take an accurate impression of your teeth. Our dental technicians need an accurate impression to make your bespoke veneers.

    5. Let Us Double-Check

    Email us pictures of your impressions, and well check theyre right. When we approve your impressions, you post them back to us. Please do not send your impressions back to us before they have been approved by a member of the instasmile team; this could delay your order and you may incur additional costs.

    6. Well Get to Work

    Once we receive your impressions, our team of dental technicians get to work. Well spend time digitally scanning, designing and hand finishing your custom-made clip-on veneers. Once theyre ready well send them to you.

    7. Clip-On Confidence

    Your clip-on veneers will arrive, and you can clip them on and start smiling with confidence!

    8. Share your smile!

    Send us your pictures, tag us on social media and show your new smile to the world!
