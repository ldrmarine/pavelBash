    Instant, Clip-On Confidence

    Getting your new instasmile is easier than you might think…

    The Process

    1. Take Your Smile Assessment

    Answer a few quick questions, and we’ll help you choose an instasmile that’s right for you.

    2. Order Online

    Once you know which instasmile you want, place your order online securely.

    3. Keep An Eye on Your Phone & Email

    We’ll message you after placing your order & keep you updated along the way.

    4: Take Your Impression

    Your impression kit will arrive in the post. Carefully follow the instructions, and take an accurate impression of your teeth. Our dental technicians need an accurate impression to make your bespoke veneers.

    5. Let Us Double-Check

    Email us pictures of your impressions, and we’ll check they’re right. When we approve your impressions, you post them back to us. Please do not send your impressions back to us before they have been approved by a member of the instasmile team; this could delay your order and you may incur additional costs.

    6. We’ll Get to Work

    Once we receive your impressions, our team of dental technicians get to work. We’ll spend time digitally scanning, designing and hand finishing your custom-made clip-on veneers. Once they’re ready we’ll send them to you.

    7. Clip-On Confidence

    Your clip-on veneers will arrive, and you can clip them on and start smiling with confidence!

    8. Share your smile!

    Send us your pictures, tag us on social media and show your new smile to the world!
